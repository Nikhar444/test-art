01 community

Snowy Mooncake Workshop with UX Designers

My roommate and I hosted a snowy mooncake workshop with UX designers for Mid-autumn Day in Bloomington, IN. People were divided into several groups and received different tasks for moon cake making, we surprisingly found some UX design methods are super helpful for this process.

We drew out the ingredients on a whiteboard which we used for practicing whiteboard challenge and presenting ideas; we assigned tasks based on everyone's interests and strength; we also didn't miss the time taking quality photos to document each progress we made.

This is an undeniably incredible experience that we created for ourselves on this family/friends-gathering festival!

02 Let's Learn and Grow Together

Mentoring First-year Designers

From Aug 2019 - Dec 2019, I participated in mentoring first-year design students at Indiana University. Most of my students do not have a background in design before and just started their design study at school. It was a challenge for all of us, I need to convey my understanding of design process and methods in an easy-to-understand language but be concise and accurate at the same time.

At the end of 2019, my 25 students successfully carried out their first design projects based on topics of fashion, respect, and news with high-quality deliverables. I deeply feel proud of them and myself!
